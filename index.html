All about us here at ICCS…

ICCS is a family-owned business who have been trading since 1992. There are 5 full time employees and several part time workers.

Alan Worthington, the proprietor, has been involved with various types of cleaning and maintenance since the age of 16. The day-to-day operations are now handled by Paul Worthington.

We have worked with Respond Housing for over 20 years and have built an excellent working relationship with a number of other businesses around the country.

Besides contract cleaning, ICCS also provide several other services such as, high reach window cleaning, carpet cleaning, office cleaning, initial house cleaning, landlord house cleaning, soft washing, grass cutting, weed killing and rubbish removal.

We are of course fully insured, and our staff are fully trained and certified for the work which they do. We are very safety conscious and take all necessary steps to protect our customers and our staff.

Alan Worthington.
